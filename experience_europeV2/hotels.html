Hotel Information

England's Top 2 Hotels:

Milestone Hotel:

Contained within a trio of historic buildings, The Milestone Hotel & Residences boasts a rich and storied history that spans several centuries.

Located in the heart of Kensington and originally built in the late 19th century, our building used to serve as a group of private residences for wealthy aristocrats.

There are several different types of rooms such as Superior Queen, Deluxe Twin or King, Deluxe Studio, Interconnecting Rooms & Suites, and Interconnecting Rooms.

For the Milestone Hotel, rooms can range from $385 to $2,223 per night depending on the type of room.

Hotel 41:

41 is one of the most distinguished and admired hotels in London, overlooking the Royal Mews and the grounds of Buckingham Palace.

A discrete and intimate hideaway, 41 has the relaxed feel of an exclusive private members club.

There are several different types of rooms such as Executive King Room, Executive Double Room, Executive Single Room and Interleading Room.

For the 41 Hotel, rooms can range from $284 to $510 for standard rooms and $1,610 and $3,580 for luxury suites.

Italy's Top 2 Hotels:

Hotel San Michele:

Located in the ancient Baldelli Palace, it has been restored with appropriate regard for the original architectural features.

The specialist press and guidebooks consider this hotel a true “Concentration of Cortona’s history” and the Italian Ministry of Art listed it as a “National Monument”.

There are several different types of rooms such as Quadruple Room, Suite Sotto IL Sole, Suite La Terrazza, Single Room, Superior Room, Classic Room, Suite La Torretta, Triple Room, Economy Room, Suite Masaccio, and Suite Belvedere.

For Hotel San Michele, rooms can range from $108 for a standard room to $511 for a deluxe room.

Hotel Panoramic:

The Hotel Panoramic is located in Montepulciano in Tuscany in the green Tuscan countryside.

with a splendid outdoor swimming pool with whirlpool with a splendid view of Montepulciano and Lake Trasimeno.

For Hotel Panoramic, rooms can range from $90 to $142.

France's Top 2 Hotels:

Le Tronçais:

The Hotel Le Tronçais is located in the heart of the Tronçais forest, an 11,000-hectare haven of greenery.

A large number of hiking trails, lakes in the heart of the forest, remarkable oaks several hundred years old, a green vault at the height of cathedrals... the sentinel, the twins, the square oak and the others are the names given to the oaks of the Tronçais woods which evoke the history of the place by their particular shape.

For the Le Tronçais prices can range from $115 to $129 a night.

Ibis Styles Bourges:

Located at the entrance to the city of Bourges, our hotel has a contemporary design combining modernity, comfort and quality.

In the summer season, enjoy the terrace overlooking the green garden, a heated outdoor swimming pool and outdoor games (table tennis, badminton and mölkky).

There are many types of rooms that a person can stay in such as standard room with 1 double bed, standard room with 2 single beds and standard family suite with 1 double bed and 2 single beds.

For the Ibis Styles Bourges prices can range from $103 to $142 a night.

Switzerland's Top 2 Hotels:

Hotel Villa Honegg:

The Hotel Villa Honegg is a unique 5-star superior boutique hotel with 23 exquisitely furnished rooms.

With its exclusive offer and exposed location, it is unique in Switzerland.

There are many different rools that someone can stay in at Hotel Villa Honegg such as Master Suite Lake View, Corner Suite, Junior Suite Alpine Panorama, Superior Room, Classic Room and Classic Room Top Floor.

For the Hotel Villa Honegg princes for rooms can range from $638 to $3,050 a night.

Hotel Edelweiss:

The opportunities and natural attractions of the Jungfrau Region are hard to top. Explore the various mountains, glaciers, gorges, water falls and the breathtaking scenery by hiking, cable cars, trains, gondolas and so on.

The possibilities are boundless. If you wish we can give you excellent advice in planning your excursions.

There are 25 rooms, all en-suite with TV, Wifi and Safe. All rooms to the south have a balcony with view to the famous Jungfrau mountain.

For the Hotel Edelweiss prices for rooms can range from $271 up to $578 a night.
