About England

England is a country rich in history, tradition, and cultural influence. As the largest nation in the United Kingdom, England blends ancient architecture with modern innovation, offering something for every type of traveler. From the vibrant streets of London to the tranquil countryside of the Cotswolds, England showcases timeless charm and cutting-edge creativity.

The capital city, London, is home to globally renowned landmarks such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and the London Eye.
